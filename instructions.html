How to use Robo-Registration:

  1. Enter your UVM Username and UVM Password to the boxes.
  2. Click the “Test Login” button to ensure that you are able to log in to the page with the given credentials.
  3. Choose the time and date that you are supposed to be registering at.
  4. Input your CRNs and their respective backups into the boxes.
  5. Ensure your computer will not sleep on its own overnight.
    NOTE: We have built a system to keep your computer awake, but we cannot ensure the fact that it will stay awake.
  6. Click "Run" and leave your computer open and unlocked overnight for Robo-Registration to work.

Steps to Make Sure Robo-Registration Works

  1. Make sure your computer is plugged in and will not sleep overnight.
    (Windows Instructions) (Mac Instructions)
  2. Ensure you do not have registration holds on your account.
